# Clarify Pipeline — Gather Context Before Optimizing

When triage selects the **Clarify** lane, the prompt is substantive but could go in meaningfully different directions. Gather context first, then proceed to the Optimize pipeline.

## When to Clarify

- The user's situation, constraints, or goals are unclear ("Should I build an MVP?" — as what? for whom? with what budget?)
- The answer would change significantly depending on unstated context
- You'd have to make assumptions that risk wasting the user's time

## How to Clarify

Use **multiple-choice questions** (2–5 options each) to make it fast to respond:

- Keep to **2–3 questions max**
- Always include a final option like **"Other — tell me more."**
- No preamble — just the choices
- Get the context, then proceed to the Optimize pipeline (`references/optimize.md`)

## Example

User: *"Should I build my startup's MVP as a mobile app or web app first?"*

Response:

> **What's the core user action?**
> a) Marketplace / transactions
> b) Content creation or consumption
> c) Real-time communication
> d) Other — tell me more
>
> **Who's your target user?**
> a) B2B — mostly on desktop at work
> b) B2C — mostly on mobile, on the go
> c) Both — use case will determine device
> d) Other — tell me more
>
> **Do you have existing traction?**
> a) Yes — existing audience or paying customers
> b) No — starting from zero
> c) Some early interest but no revenue yet
> d) Other — tell me more

*User picks options, then proceed to the Optimize pipeline with that context.*
